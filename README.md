# 🛍️ Modern AI Shop

**Modern AI Shop** is a full-stack e-commerce platform for discovering futuristic gadgets across a range of categories, complete with detailed product listings, images, and pricing. It's powered by two AI features: an **AI Spec Generator** that instantly drafts complete product listings from a short concept description, and a **site-wide AI Chat Assistant** that helps users navigate the platform and answer product questions in real time — no login required.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 1. 🔐 Authentication (Email & Google)
Users can sign up and log in using their email and password, or instantly access the platform via **Google OAuth** — making onboarding fast and frictionless.

### 2. 🛒 Product Catalog
Browse a wide range of products across categories, with detailed listings including images, pricing, and descriptions.

### 3. 🤖 AI Spec Generator
Powered by Google's Gemini API, this tool instantly generates complete product listings — title, category, price, summary, and detailed specifications — from a short description of the product concept. Supports adjustable tone and output length, with a one-click regenerate option.

### 4. 💬 AI Chat Assistant
A site-wide conversational assistant that helps users navigate the platform, answer product questions, and maintain context across the conversation — offering real-time, human-like support without needing to log in.

### 5. 🔍 Smart Search & Filtering
Find products quickly with keyword search combined with filters for category, price range, and ratings.

### 6. 📦 Item Management
Users can view their submitted product history, inspect individual listings in detail, and delete items they no longer want listed.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Hero UI
- **Backend:** Node.js / Express
- **Auth:** Better Auth (Email + Google OAuth)
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **AI:** Recommendation engine (gemini-3.1-flash-lite)
